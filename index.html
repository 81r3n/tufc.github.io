about TUFC

Transport United Football Club (TUFC) is a Bhutanese professional football club based in Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan and competes in the Bhutan Premier League. The club was founded in 2001 and plays at the  Changlimithang Stadium .Transport United Football Club was established by the Road Safety and Transport Authority (RSTA) and volunteers from the private transporters in 2001. RSTA and the volunteers backed the club until the club got relegated to the second division in 2007. The present Club General Secretary, Technical Director and the treasurer continued with the club and fought for promotion until 2016. Read more..
