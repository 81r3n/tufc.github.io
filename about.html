our corporate social responsibility

Intro

Football Clubs in Bhutan are progressively increasing their level of complexity in terms of resources, organization and strategies. These days, the clubs in Bhutan are not only interested in how to win matches but we also owe a sense of decency to our fans. Because we are the ambassador of this beautiful game to our fans and audience, we are in a privileged position to influence them positively and vice versa. Transport United FC (TUFC) has been heavily influenced by the society and by the local community. In its activity, the club takes over values of the community of which it is a member and reproduces them onto the football pitch or in each ever other activities it runs. Hence, the CSR of the club was born.

Our CSR

Our primary responsibility to our community is to take the youth off the street and engage them in the football pitches. During the course of the year, we organize various soccer camps for the youth and by the youth. We train trainers and players for free as an appreciation to our society and show that we care. We involve interested parents to join such camps as trainers to encourage social involvement which is very important for the club’s CSR to be successful. These camps conducted by the club not only created a healthy society but also helped the youth by training them the capacity to reflect upon themselves and the surrounding world, giving them a better perception of the society and improving their self-esteem. The club has also adopted Druk School in Thimphu as part of our CSR and offer educational programs for shaping the next generation. The proactive movement of the clubs can be explained through the fact that a better skilled and educated society may bring up football fans of a higher value, or even future footballers, for the club. Our other responsibility is using the club’s popular social media handles and images to disseminate important messages to the public. We make sure we are in touch with the relevant agencies to convey their messages to the public. Our campaign so far has included educating on Menstrual Hygiene, Covid Protocol, Blood donation drives and more.

Conclusion

At TUFC, we believe that by practicing CSR activities, the club strengthens its bond with the fans to form a community, therefore the devotion of the fans for the football club increases. The increased devotion is one of the most important returns on CSR investment for the club. Over the long run, the club can capitalize upon the devotion and sell more and better to the fans, thus raising its incomes.

about TUFC

Transport United Football Club (TUFC) is a Bhutanese professional football club based in Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan and competes in the Bhutan Premier League. The club was founded in 2001 and plays at the  Changlimithang Stadium .Transport United Football Club was established by the Road Safety and Transport Authority (RSTA) and volunteers from the private transporters in 2001. RSTA and the volunteers backed the club until the club got relegated to the second division in 2007. The present Club General Secretary, Technical Director and the treasurer continued with the club and fought for promotion until 2016.

Our CSR

mission

To promote football as the ultimate sports activity for the competitive and social needs of the people of Bhutan.

Give equal opportunity to everyone to develop and thrive in this beautiful game.

Play football with a competitive spirit and good sportmanship 

vision

TUFC is devoted to fostering and providing a high quality soccer environment in order to give all the players, coaches, referees and managers every opportunity to enjoy the game of soccer and to help them achieve their maximum potential as participants in this sport, while at all times promoting a sense of fair play.
